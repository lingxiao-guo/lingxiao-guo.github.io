I am a first-year Master student at Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Guanya Shi. I received my Bachelor degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (2025). Previously, I was fortunate to be advised by Huazhe Xu at Tsinghua IIIS.

My research interests lie in the intersection of Robotics, Reinforcement Learning and Self-Supervised Learning. I focus on building generalizable and agile robot systems that can continually learn in the open world.
